Claims procedure sample

A claim normally starts with notification to the insurer. The notice should include the policy number, date of loss, location, description of the event, parties involved, and immediate steps taken.

The insurer may request a completed claim form, proof of loss, invoices, photographs, police report, repair estimates, medical records, or other supporting documents.

If a claim is denied, the policyholder should request the denial reason in writing, compare it with the policy wording, gather missing evidence, and submit an appeal or complaint before any deadline.

Claim decisions depend on coverage grants, exclusions, conditions, limits, deductibles, and whether the policyholder met notification and cooperation duties.
