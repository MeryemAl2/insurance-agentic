Insurance regulation sample

Insurers should handle claims fairly, communicate material reasons for claim decisions, and provide complaint or appeal routes where required by applicable regulation.

Renewal terms may change based on underwriting rules, claim history, regulatory limits, and notice requirements. The insurer should disclose important changes before renewal where required.

Policyholders should read notices carefully and compare revised deductibles, limits, exclusions, premiums, and covered uses before accepting renewal.
