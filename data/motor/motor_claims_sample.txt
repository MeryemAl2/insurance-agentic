Motor insurance sample

Comprehensive motor insurance may cover accidental damage to the insured vehicle, theft, fire, and third-party liability, subject to the policy wording.

After a road accident, the policyholder should make the scene safe, call emergency services if anyone is injured, exchange details with the other driver, take photos, and notify the insurer as soon as possible.

Common motor claim documents include the claim form, policy number, driver license, vehicle registration, accident photos, police report where required, repair estimate, and contact details of third parties or witnesses.

Exclusions may apply when the vehicle is used outside the permitted use, when the driver is unlicensed, when the driver is impaired, or when the loss is intentional.
